Client Onboarding

Welcome to Ames 2 Please Taxes. This page outlines what you can expect when working with me, along with a checklist of documents you may need for your tax preparation. My goal is to make this process clear, calm, and accessible for every client.

Client Expectations

  • You will receive clear communication and step‑by‑step guidance throughout the process.
  • Appointments will be paced to support your communication and processing needs.
  • You may request written summaries, slower pacing, or breaks at any time.
  • All documents must be submitted through the secure portal unless otherwise arranged.
  • Turnaround times may vary during peak tax season; I will always communicate realistic timelines.
  • You will be notified promptly if additional information or clarification is needed.
  • Respectful, judgment‑free support is provided to all clients, including neurodiverse individuals.

Document Checklist

Please gather any documents that apply to your situation:

  • W‑2 forms from all employers
  • 1099 forms (NEC, MISC, INT, DIV, K, R, SSA, etc.)
  • Self‑employment income and expense records
  • Business mileage logs
  • Investment statements or composite reports
  • Retirement income statements (1099‑R)
  • Social Security statements
  • Education forms (1098‑T, 1098‑E)
  • Childcare provider statements
  • Health insurance forms (1095‑A, B, or C)
  • Property tax statements
  • Mortgage interest statements (1098)
  • Charitable donation receipts
  • IRS or state tax letters received during the year
  • Last year’s tax return (if you are a new client)

If you’re unsure whether a document is needed, feel free to ask — I’m happy to help you determine what applies to your situation.
