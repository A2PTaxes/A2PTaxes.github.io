Onboarding

Welcome to Ames 2 Please Taxes. This page will guide you through the onboarding process so you know exactly what to expect and how to prepare for your appointment.

Step 1: Book Your Appointment

Use the contact page to request an appointment. I’ll follow up with available times and next steps.

Step 2: Gather Your Documents

  • W‑2s, 1099s, and income statements
  • Expense summaries (if self‑employed)
  • Last year’s tax return (optional but helpful)
  • Any IRS or Oregon letters

Step 3: Complete the Intake Form

Before your appointment, you’ll receive a secure intake form to help me understand your situation and prepare efficiently.

Step 4: Your Appointment

We’ll meet virtually or in person to review your documents, answer questions, and complete your return with clarity and care.

Step 5: Filing & Follow‑Up

Once your return is ready, I’ll review it with you before filing. You’ll receive a copy for your records and support for any follow‑up questions.
