About Samantha Ames

Hi, I’m Samantha — an Oregon Licensed Tax Consultant, educator, creative strategist, and the owner of Ames 2 Please Taxes. My mission is simple: to make taxes feel clear, calm, and human for every person who walks through my (virtual or physical) door.

I believe tax preparation should never feel intimidating or judgmental. Whether you’re filing your first return, navigating self‑employment, or managing a small business, you deserve support that meets you where you are — with patience, clarity, and respect.

My Approach

My work blends technical expertise with a warm, accessible teaching style. I specialize in helping:

  • Neurodiverse individuals who need pacing, structure, and clarity
  • First‑time filers who want to understand the process
  • Self‑employed and gig workers navigating complex rules
  • Small business owners seeking confidence and organization
  • Anyone who has ever felt overwhelmed, ashamed, or confused by taxes

I believe that understanding your taxes is empowering — and that everyone deserves a safe, judgment‑free space to learn.

Professional Background

I am licensed by the Oregon Board of Tax Practitioners (LTC#1922362‑C) and operate as an IRS Electronic Return Originator (ERO). I stay current on federal and state tax law, compliance requirements, and best practices to ensure every return is prepared with accuracy and care.

Beyond the Numbers

Outside of tax season, I’m a creative educator, public speaker, and advocate for financial literacy. I’m also the author of Slice by Slice: Growing Up With Taxes, a book designed to help young people understand money and taxes through storytelling, humor, and real‑life lessons.

Whether I’m preparing a return, teaching a workshop, or helping someone untangle a confusing IRS letter, my goal is always the same: to make the process feel lighter, clearer, and more manageable.
