About the Preparer

Ames 2 Please Taxes is owned and operated by Samantha Ames, an Oregon‑licensed Tax Consultant (LTC#1922362‑C), Registered PTIN holder, Oregon Notary Public, and authorized IRS ERO.

Samantha blends technical expertise with a deeply human approach to financial literacy, meeting clients where they are — literally and emotionally. Whether you’re filing a simple return, navigating a complex tax situation, or building a new business, you’ll receive guidance that is practical, judgment‑free, and tailored to your needs.

Ames 2 Please Taxes is built on the belief that financial empowerment should feel accessible, safe, and even a little magical.
