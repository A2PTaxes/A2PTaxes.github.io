Public Speaking & Workshops

I offer engaging, accessible presentations on taxes, small business topics, and financial literacy. My speaking style blends clarity, humor, and real‑world examples to make complex topics feel understandable — and even enjoyable.

Signature Keynotes

These talks are designed for schools, nonprofits, small businesses, and community groups. Each keynote can be adapted for different audiences and timeframes.

  • “Slice by Slice: Growing Up With Taxes” A storytelling‑driven keynote about financial literacy, empowerment, and the journey from confusion to confidence.
  • “Taming the Tax Dragons” A whimsical, fantasy‑infused talk that helps audiences understand taxes through approachable metaphors and humor.
  • “Small Business, Big Clarity” A practical keynote for entrepreneurs who want to understand taxes, recordkeeping, and business structure without overwhelm.
