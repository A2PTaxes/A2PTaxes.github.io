Public Speaking & Workshops

I offer engaging, accessible workshops and speaking engagements designed to make taxes and financial literacy feel clear, empowering, and even enjoyable. My teaching style blends storytelling, humor, and real‑world examples to help audiences connect with information that often feels intimidating.

My Approach

Every workshop is designed to be warm, judgment‑free, and accessible to a wide range of learning styles. I prioritize clarity, pacing, and real‑life examples that help people feel confident and capable.
