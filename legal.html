Legal & Privacy

Ames 2 Please Taxes is committed to transparency, professionalism, and compliance with all applicable state and federal regulations. This page outlines key legal and privacy information related to my services.

Licensing & Compliance

  • Oregon Licensed Tax Consultant (LTC#1922362‑C)
  • Registered with the Oregon Board of Tax Practitioners
  • IRS Electronic Return Originator (ERO)
  • Annual continuing education completed as required by state and federal law

Privacy & Confidentiality

Your information is treated with the highest level of confidentiality. I follow all applicable IRS, federal, and Oregon privacy regulations, including:

  • Secure handling of tax documents
  • No sharing of personal information without explicit consent
  • Secure electronic filing procedures
  • Encrypted communication methods when applicable

Document Retention

Tax documents are retained in accordance with IRS and Oregon Board of Tax Practitioners requirements. You may request copies of your documents at any time.

Limitations of Services

I provide tax preparation, education, and general guidance. I do not provide legal advice or financial investment advice. For matters outside my scope, I may recommend consulting an attorney or financial professional.

Website & Digital Privacy

This website does not sell or share personal information. Any data submitted through the contact form is used solely for communication and service coordination.
