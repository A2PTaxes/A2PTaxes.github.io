Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Thank you for choosing our tax consultancy services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Personal Information: This includes your name, contact details, Social Security Number (SSN), tax-related documents, and financial information.

Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with our website, emails, and other communication channels.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies: Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes

Tax Preparation and Filing: We process your personal information to prepare and file your tax returns accurately.

Communication: We use your contact details to communicate with you regarding your tax matters.

Improving Services: We analyze usage data to enhance our services and tailor them to your needs.

Legal Compliance: We may share information as required by law or in response to legal requests.

4. Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your data:

Encryption: We use encryption protocols to secure data transmission.

Access Control: Only authorized personnel have access to your information.

Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits to identify vulnerabilities.

5. Third Parties

WE DO NOT SELL YOUR INFORMATION

We may share your information with:

Tax Authorities: To comply with legal obligations.

Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist us in providing our services.

Legal Entities: In case of mergers, acquisitions, or other business transitions.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

Access: Request access to your personal information.

Correction: Correct any inaccuracies in your data.

Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements).

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at questions@samanthaames.online.

***DO NOT SEND ANY PERSONAL IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION (PII) VIA EMAIL OR OVER THE PHONE. INSTEAD REQUEST A TAX PORTAL LINK***
