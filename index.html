Privacy policy

1. Introduction

Thank you for choosing Ames 2 Please Taxes for your tax and consulting needs. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

2. Information we collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information: Your name, contact details, and limited tax-related information as needed to provide our services.
  • Usage data: Information about how you interact with our website, emails, and other communication channels.
  • Cookies and tracking technologies: Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience.

3. How we use your information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Tax preparation and filing, where applicable and authorized.
  • Communication regarding your tax matters, appointments, and services.
  • Improving our services and tailoring them to your needs.
  • Legal compliance and responding to lawful requests.

4. Data security

We take appropriate measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption and secure transmission through our MyTaxOffice client portal for tax documents.
  • Access control so that only authorized personnel can access your information.
  • Regular reviews of our practices to identify and address vulnerabilities.

5. Third parties

We do not sell your information.

We may share your information with:

  • Tax authorities, to comply with legal obligations.
  • Service providers who assist in delivering our services (e.g., secure portals, software providers).
  • Legal entities in the event of mergers, acquisitions, or similar business transitions.

6. Your rights

You may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of certain data, subject to legal retention requirements.

7. Contact us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at questions@samanthaames.online.

Important: Do not send any personally identifiable information (PII), tax documents, or Social Security Numbers via email or over the phone. Instead, request a secure MyTaxOffice portal link for all tax document uploads and sensitive information.
