Pricing

My pricing is transparent, straightforward, and designed to meet you where you are. Every return is unique, so final pricing depends on the forms and schedules required — but the ranges below will help you understand what to expect.

Individual Tax Returns

  • Simple Return (W‑2 only): $125–$175
  • Standard Return: $175–$275
    Includes common forms such as unemployment, Social Security, interest, dividends, and basic credits.
  • Itemized Return: $250–$350
    Includes Schedule A and related documentation.
  • Self‑Employed / Gig Work: $300–$500
    Includes Schedule C, mileage logs, and expense summaries.

Small Business & Advanced Returns

  • Single‑Member LLC: $350–$600
  • Multi‑Member LLC (Partnership): $600–$900
  • Bookkeeping Cleanup (optional): $50–$100/hr
  • Business Formation Assistance: $150–$300

Additional Services

  • Amended Return: $150–$300
  • Extension Filing: $25
  • Tax Letters / Notices: $50–$150
    Includes review and recommended next steps.
  • Audit Support / Representation: $100/hr
  • Notary Services: $10 per signature

Mobile Appointments

Mobile appointments are available within Klamath Falls and surrounding areas.

  • In‑Town Mobile Appointment: $25 travel fee
  • Out‑of‑Town Travel: Quoted based on distance

Payment Options

  • Cash, card, and electronic payments accepted
  • Payment is due at the time of filing
  • Invoices available upon request

No Surprises Policy

You’ll always know your estimated price before we begin. If your return becomes more complex than expected, I’ll explain why and discuss options with you before continuing.
