Accessibility

Ames 2 Please Taxes is committed to providing a supportive, inclusive, and accessible experience for every client. I believe that financial clarity should be available to everyone, and I work to ensure that my services meet a wide range of needs.

Communication & Processing Support

  • Clear, step‑by‑step explanations
  • Flexible pacing during appointments
  • Breaks available at any time
  • Written summaries upon request

Neurodiversity‑Inclusive Practices

  • Judgment‑free environment
  • Predictable structure and expectations
  • Options for visual, verbal, or written communication
  • Support for executive functioning challenges

Physical & Sensory Accommodations

  • Virtual appointments available
  • Low‑stimulus communication options
  • Flexible scheduling for energy and focus needs

Document Accessibility

Forms and documents can be provided in accessible formats upon request. If you need help gathering or understanding your tax documents, I can guide you through the process step by step.

Let Me Know What You Need

If you have specific accessibility needs, please reach out. I’m happy to adapt my process to support you.
